About Me

Hello! My name is Nishanth and I am a Cloud Solutions Architect at the UCSC, where I design and implement cloud-based solutions for various academic and research projects. With over 12 years of experience in cloud computing, cloud architecture, DevOps engineering, and Linux systems administration.

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at Centene Corporation, Verizon, UHG, and Viacom, (then Viacom, now Paramount Global). My main focus these days is to deliver scalable, reliable, and secure cloud solutions that meet the needs and expectations of a variety of clients.

Here are a few technologies I’ve been working with recently:

  • AWS Solutions Architecture
  • Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
  • Serverless Architecture (Lambda, API Gateway)
  • Kubernetes (EKS)
  • CI/CD Pipelines (Jenkins, GitLab CI/CD)
  • AWS Networking (VPC, Route 53, CloudFront)
  • Security and Compliance (IAM, AWS Config, Amazon Inspector)
  • Monitoring and Logging (CloudWatch, Prometheus, Grafana)
  • Cost Optimization (AWS Budgets, Trusted Advisor)
  • Python & Scripting for Automation
